PREFACE

The Vedas and Upanishads are considered as the greatest mystrical expressions of the highest spiritual wisdom of Indian culture.The Lifestyle of creature,Co-operation of living togeather,Goal of Life,Creation of this Universe all these thoughts are found in the Vedas.
                                 Vedas are considered as 'APAURUSHAYA' (Impersonal,authorless). It is Universal Truth  .Every one should follow the Principles of the Vedas and Upanishads.







 
                                      
                    

INTRODUCTION



Before 3rd century Vedas were illegible (unable to read). observing the limited perseverance, energy and application of mortals, Lord Vishnu makes the Veda fourfold, to adapt it to     their capacities ;   and the bodily form which he assumes ,in order to effect that classification, is known by the name of Veda-vyasa.

The main aim of this classification is to study all the Vedas, but it is unable to understand Upanishads and Mahakavyas. When we read it, we face uncertainty. So these doubts are clarified by great Philosopher , Shri Madhwacharya in his literature. These writings are known as ‘Sarvamoola Granthas’ .If we follow his principles, his study, it is easy to understand Vedas and Upanishads. Hence we started study centre by the name  “Shree Vyasa Madhwa Samskruta Vidhyalaya”.

                                     Every day, Pandit Vedanidhi Acharya, the spiritul guider gives spiritual training to us in this study centre by his thoughts, God has impressed and therefore he came from Nepal in the form of 'Shaaligrama shila' ,which consists of Saptadhatu which is visible.  Since from ancient days Vyasa Mahabharata ,written by Vyasa is considered as "Yadasthi Tadann Yatra ,Yennehasti Kutrchitt".  While preaching the Upanyasa about this,the god himself choosen the place where he should reside.

                                  One day Dr.Upendra Narsapur visited vidhyalaya for some work and impressed by the idol of Lord Vishnu and he made sankalpa for Bhudana. According to chandopanishattu 'Aagamanarthu devanam,palayanantu asuranam'.Before placing this idol, we faced so many problems.when Shri Shri 1008  Shri Satyathmateertha Swamiji visited site then all the problems were eradicated.Then it was easy to construct "Vishnu Sahasranama Bhavanam".
