--> Indian historical hymn Vishnusahasranama engraved on the stones will be installed.

-->The video clips comprising of posters and information of various places of pilgrimage found in the north,south,east,west of Indian peninsular will be provided to the devotees who can comfortably perceive the sacred places sitting in the premise of the building.



-->Availing the guidance and expertise of reputed architects and engineers a new huge magnanimous edifice with a modern touch spectacular Vishnusahasranama Bhavan will be in addition to the historical monumental buildings of Adilshahi regime that will really safeguard the ancient heritage.

-->Students coming from different areas of the city intresyed to learn Sanskrit will be thought Sanskrit.



-->A library and a research centre will be set up for those who want to study Sanskrit and Kannada and many other books.

-->A sacred serene, peaceful abode of divine presence of Lord Venkatesh will be founded for the benefit of all and a place of prayer wherein devotees may have soothing experience of mental peace in the devotional centre.






-->Devotes, achievers can perform their chanting of hyms and other religious rituals freely.

-->A unique place of faith and devotion gets its shape here.
