A unique creation of Shree Vishnusahasra Nama Bhavan that could create a new history for the ages to come

Prayer is the spiritual practice, prayer generates good spiritual currents and proves tranquillity of the mind. In the material world filled with din, dust and noise ,Temple is the place with peaceful environment for concentration.The gist of all shastras and Mahabharata’s essence is Vishnusahasra Nama engraved on the stones will have its existence for all times.

With the gracious goodness of Lord Shree Hari-Vayu-Gurus and with the kindness of his holiness Uttaradimathadeesh Shri Shri 1008 Shri Satyatmateertha Shreepadaji and with the blessings of Vidyaguru learned Shree Mahuli Vidyasimhachrya and erudite Shree Madhvacharya Mokashi along with all pious personalities,psalm groups(bhajane groups) and under the apt, able guidance of all at Vijayapur on Torvi road B.D.A site R.S.S.746/E,C.A has been sanctioned to shree Vyasa Madhva Sanskrit Vidyalaya to conduct religious activities for the benefit of the society at large.

                                                                 In the said site a beautiful edifice of Shree Vishnusahasra nama bhavan will take its shape in the inner shrine (interior) of the attractive temple Nepal’s six feet height God Shree Venkatesh’s statue’s benediction(adhishtan) will be installed.
                                         
                                                                In this serene,calm and quite abode of almighty religious sermons, preaching’s will be delivered in order to popularise Vyasa and Dasa literature and to imbibe and inculate human values to uplift the mankind.
       
                                                                  It has been our earnest,intent desire to propagate Indian culture and heritage to elevate human race. 
        THIS TEMPLE ALSO CONSISTS OF RESEARCH CENTER NAMED AS                 " SHREE VYASA MADHWA VIDYALAYA RESEARCH CENTER".
