VIGRAHAM
Vigraha is an image or an material object representing a deity to which religious worship is addressed or any person or thing regarded with admiration ,adoration, or devotion.
The Temple is dedicated to Lord Sri Venkateswara, an incarnation of Vishnu, who is believed to have appeared here to save mankind from trials and troubles of Kali Yuga.
According to the Brahmanda and Bhavishyottara Puranas, the word "Venkata" means "destroyer of sins", deriving from the Sanskrit words vem (sins) and kata (power of immunity).

Description of Vigraha

 Four people namely Pandit Vedanidhi Acharya,P.R.Srinivas,Ganapatibhatt Manchali and Dr.Kanad went to kashi from Uttaradi Math  Bangalore with blessings of Shri Shri 1008 Shri Satyatmateertha Swamiji and they returned on Ashada Krishna Paksha Dashami.

This stone was taken from River Gandaki near Nepal to kashi by Shivanand Swaroop Swamy and was stone Sculptured by Anuj Gaur.
