Choukat  pooja Photos

Vastu governs positive and negative energy inside the house. Positive zones such as east, north and north-east give us positive energy for working. These zones should be used for pooja room in the house.

The Main Door and the Main Door Frame are considered as places of sanctity where Goddess Mahalakshmi resides. This Door Frameshould be installed with enough of sanctity and devotion and should be maintained with care. 

There is a procedure which should be followed in installing the Main Door Frame. The Main Door Frame is the wooden frame already manufactured and kept ready for installation on the opening in the wall, earmarked for the Main Door.
When the Vaasthu time commences, perform Neivedyam to the Main Door Frame, show managalarati, pray Lord Maha Ganapathi and Goddess Mahalashmi, spread the Panchaloga and Navaratna pieces on the wet cement on the base of the Doorway, fix the Maha Ganapathi Dollar and Mahalakshmi Dollar on the side bottoms of the Doorway, spread the 5 rupee coins on the entire stretch of the base of the Doorway in a line.
